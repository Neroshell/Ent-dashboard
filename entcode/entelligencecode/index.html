About Us

e.ntelligence

We develop software and hardware solutions to improve remote distributed renewable energy systems in Africa. With a strong focus on monitoring, intelligent operations, and flexible billing, our aim is to empower energy developers to overcome the challenges of energy access faced by the global south.

  • Flexible billing
  • Remote monitoring
  • Intelligent operations
  • Tailored solutions

Services

Flexible billing

Through our innovative solution, we enable energy developers to serve customers with and without bank accounts and debit cards, with or without access to stable internet, bridging the gap for all members of the community.

Remote monitoring

We offer hardware interface and robust software to collect generation and consumption data from remote distributed energy systems.

Intelligent operations

Our software offers data analytics capabilities, allowing off-grid energy developers to optimize their operations, reduce losses, and improve energy efficiency.

Tailored solutions

We collaborate with off-grid and on-grid energy developers to tailor solutions to their specific needs.
